Frequently Asked Questions

What is StartStaying ?

Our company StartStaying helps provide singles or families with homes on rent. The rental agreement takes place between the homeowner and the tenant and StartStaying acts on behalf of the owner. Two type of homes are available for rent as of now:

  • Fully furnished homes are ready to move in homes, where one can book a single bed or private room or a complete home on rent.
    • In the shared homes , the hall/dining and kitchen area are shared among all the housemates.
  • Semi furnished homes can be booked as a complete home only .

 

Where do these homes come from ?

We get homes from homeowners like you who like to get more rental and not have the headache of rental process and maintenance thereafter. There has been a lot of interest from homeowners and tenants for our properties. Don't lose out !

How does a house get listed on StartStaying ?

 

  • Owner approaches Startstaying to list the house
  • StartStaying assesses property
  • An agreement is done
  • Call 080-30695768 or leave a message  "Click here"

 

How do we do it ?

We standardize the renting process of a home

  • We maintain a standard in the homes we pick .
  • We advise on the furnishings that need to be present in the home.
  • We have all home maintenance staff with us who ready the house. We take pictures and show it on our website.
  • We reach the prospective tenants by marketing through various media channels .
  • We have standard practices for security deposit thus making it easier for the tenant to make a decision.
By making a win-win deal for both tenants and owners, the house remains occupied for the most period. This gets you more rent along with our professional services. Not to mention the time you save because of our efficient services.

 

How long does it take to onboard a house on StartStaying

 

  • StartStaying lists the property on portal in 15 days once the agreement is done.
  • StartStaying takes 1-2 month time to fill the beds on best effort basis depending on the location.
  •  

 

How much rental will I get?

We have seen the rentals to be 5-40% higher in some cases. But we do not promise anything. We try our best to get you the best rentals.

  • For fully -furnished homes which have been put for per bed rental basis, the rental is the sum of rents received from all the individuals living in the home in that month.We take care of rental defaulters.
  • For semi-furnished homes, as these are rented out as a whole, the rental will be based on how many days the property is occupied.

 

Will StartStaying ensure guaranteed monthly rentals ?

StartStaying provides rental as per the occupancy. StartStaying tries best to keep the occupancy rate at 100% as we get our service charges only on the rental you are getting. So it is in our best interest as well to fill your property and keep it occupied for most time.

What are the service charges of StartStaying ?

 

Our service charges are very affordable. Please call us and you will be surprised. These charges are deducted and the rent per month is transferred by 7th of every month for the previous month. Let's say you get rent for Feb 2017 by 7th March 2017.

 

What is the probability that the property remains vacant ?
We take homes which are closer to work places, educational institutions, hospitals etc. These will not remain vacant as there is a huge demand for such properties. We have seen an average occupancy of 90% for a home in our experience .
What is a semi -furnished home?
A semi-furnished home is one which has lockable woodwork in all bedrooms & has woodwork in kitchen. Geysers, light fixtures, fans , door bell, mirrors, basic bathroom fittings should be present.
What is a fully-furnished home?
  • Hall : 5 seater Sofa, center table, TV stand, 32 inch Flatscreen TV ,Large Shoe Stand
  • Dining: Dining table with chairs.
  • Kitchen: Dining set, Pressure Cookers, Pots n Pans, cutlery etc.
  • Bedrooms: Wooden beds with back rest panel, mattresses, bed side table, bedsheets, pillows.
  • Bathrooms: Geysers , buckets, soap brushes stands.
  • In all rooms: Wifi router, tatasky cable Set-top box, Curtains, footmats, Mirrors
Everything that makes moving in as easy as getting just clothes. The above is an indicative list . Complete list will be provided .
What is the typical cost of furnishing ?

Please call us to know more.

Who pays the flat maintainance?

Owner has to pay the society maintainance, sinking fund or any other charges asked by the society for their flat.

What happens when plumbing ,electrical or carpentenry issues arise in the flat?

The tenant calls us for these issues and we send quality professionals to help them with these. If the issues turn out to be small or due to the general wear n tear then tenants will pay for these.
If the issues are larger then we refer these issues to you. You could then decide to let us handle it with the expenses being deducted from the rent or you can handle it yourself.
We request that all issues need to be attended as soon as possible so that the tenants do not feel the inconvenience.
In the first 2 months after the agreement has been made with the owner , all issues need to be handled with owners expenses as these could be because of faulty items that did not work in the first place. These happen mostly in new houses where there are teething issues.

Can I say that my flat be given only to families ?

We urge the owners to not have too many restrictions to whom the flat can be given out. This will reduce the number of people who will come to rent the flat. But if you want it only that way then we will take care of giving the flat to only families.
Singles are mostly those kids coming to town to work in good companies and need a good place to live. They are building blocks of our nation and let's be considerate to them by providing homes on rent.

Can I suggest the rent to be charged for house ?

Yes of course.

Can I see the rental agreement ?

Yes of course. Call us.

Will I deal with rental defaulters ?

No,We transfer the rent even if the rentees do not pay it . We take care of rental defaulters.

Can I take my house back ?

Yes, with 2 months notice period which should be informed at the start of a calendar month.

Who will take care of the maintenance of the my property

 

  • Society maintenance charges will be borne by the House owners
  • House maintenance will be taken care by Tenants.
  • However, StartStaying will ensure that the property is maintained through inspections.

 

Will StartStaying work with Apartment Association for any approvals of the property with StartStaying ?
Most associations have no issues with property management firms taking care of the properties. But owners need to be aware of the rules and regulation of the Apartment Association and should share the details with us. Inform us about the special move in, move out procedures and any other rules that the tenants need to follow. We will take care to inform the tenant regarding the same.
If I have furnished the house, who takes care of the soft furnishings in the house
StartStaying will assess the damage to the furnishing. If the damage is due to improper usage of the furnishing or wishful damaging then tenants will bear any repair charges
If the damage is due to wear and tear, owner is responsible for the repairs or replacement.
What will be the final terms of agreeement ?
    • All the terms in the website are indicative and the terms mentioned and agreed upon in the agreement will be final and binding on all the concerned parties.
